# Plan To Build A Database Of 1000 Federer Companies

## Objective

Create a verified database of 1000 Federer Companies across India within one month.

## Phase 1 - Company Discovery

Sources:

* LinkedIn
* IndiaMART
* TradeIndia
* ExportersIndia
* Industry Associations
* MCA Database
* Tracxn
* Startup Databases

Expected Output:
5000+ candidate companies.

## Phase 2 - Automated Filtering

Apply automated rules:

* Manufacturer only
* Indian operations
* Revenue range filtering
* Industry filtering
* Ownership filtering

Expected Output:
2500 companies.

## Phase 3 - AI Assisted Research

Use AI to:

* Summarize companies
* Extract leadership details
* Identify growth signals
* Detect differentiation indicators

Expected Output:
1500 companies.

## Phase 4 - Manual Verification

Research team verifies:

* Revenue evidence
* Manufacturing evidence
* Leadership evidence
* Growth evidence
* Systems maturity evidence

Expected Output:
1000 verified companies.

## Quality Control Process

Each company must satisfy:

1. Producer Criteria
2. Accessibility Criteria
3. Differentiation Criteria
4. Growth Criteria
5. Systems Maturity Criteria
6. Leadership Criteria

## Hallucination Prevention

* Multi-source verification.
* Human review before qualification.
* Missing data marked as Needs Verification.
* Maintain rejection log for failed companies.

## Estimated Timeline

Week 1:
Build raw database.

Week 2:
Automated filtering.

Week 3:
AI-assisted enrichment.

Week 4:
Manual verification and final scoring.
