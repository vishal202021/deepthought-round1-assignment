# Research Methodology

## Objective
Identify Pune-based Federer companies in:
1. Industrial Sensors & Instrumentation
2. Precision Auto Components & Engineering

## Sources Used
- Company Websites
- LinkedIn
- IndiaMART
- ExportersIndia
- Tracxn
- ZaubaCorp
- TradeIndia

## Qualification Process

Step 1: Build initial company list

Step 2: Apply producer filter
- Manufacturer
- Not distributor
- Not trader

Step 3: Apply location filter
- Pune based companies only

Step 4: Apply ownership filter
- Exclude subsidiaries
- Exclude large conglomerates

Step 5: Apply revenue filter
- Target ₹50 Cr – ₹500 Cr

Step 6: Score companies using Federer framework

## AI Hallucination Controls

- Cross-checked data across multiple sources
- Rejected unsupported claims
- Marked missing information as "Needs Verification"
- Maintained separate fail list for rejected companies
- Did not estimate revenue without evidence

## Limitations

Many private manufacturing SMEs do not publicly disclose revenue data. Rather than fabricate information, such companies were marked as Needs Verification.
