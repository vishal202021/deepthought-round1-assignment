# Sourcing Strategy

## Objective

Identify promoter-led manufacturing companies that fit the Federer Company profile.

Target Segments:

1. Industrial Sensors & Instrumentation
2. Precision Auto Components & Engineering

Target Geography:
Pune, Maharashtra

## Sources Used

### Company Websites

Used to verify:

* Products manufactured
* Manufacturing facilities
* Certifications
* Leadership information

### LinkedIn

Used to verify:

* Employee count
* Hiring activity
* Leadership accessibility
* Growth indicators

### IndiaMART

Used to verify:

* Manufacturer status
* Product catalog
* Industry positioning

### ExportersIndia

Used to identify:

* Export-oriented manufacturers
* Specialized product companies

### Tracxn

Used to verify:

* Revenue estimates
* Funding activity
* Growth indicators

### ZaubaCorp

Used to verify:

* Company existence
* Incorporation details
* Corporate information

### TradeIndia

Used to cross-check:

* Product categories
* Manufacturing capabilities

## Qualification Process

Step 1:
Build an initial list of companies from multiple sources.

Step 2:
Apply Producer Filter.
Reject traders and distributors.

Step 3:
Apply Geography Filter.
Retain Pune-based companies only.

Step 4:
Apply Revenue Filter.
Target companies between ₹50 Cr and ₹500 Cr.

Step 5:
Apply Ownership Filter.
Reject large conglomerate subsidiaries.

Step 6:
Evaluate using Federer Scoring Framework.

## Hallucination Controls

* Cross verification across multiple sources.
* Revenue marked as "Needs Verification" when unavailable.
* Rejected unsupported claims.
* Maintained separate fail list.
* Avoided assumptions without evidence.
